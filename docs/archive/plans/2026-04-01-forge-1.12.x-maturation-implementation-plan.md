## Risks And Assumptions

- Risk: `1.11.x` hardening regressions reopen while `1.12.x` is in progress
  Mitigation: pause the maturity slice, fix the regression first, then restack the affected work
- Risk: release-tier naming expands faster than behavior differences justify
  Mitigation: collapse synonyms for `1.12.0` and keep compatibility aliases explicit
- Risk: adoption-check starts drifting toward analytics or organization-level escalation
  Mitigation: keep the contract bounded to impact, confidence, friction, and next action inside Forge's release tail
- Risk: adapter cleanup starts inventing host-local policy
  Mitigation: freeze the core compatibility story first and require overlay plus generated-artifact tests before and after the cleanup
- Assumption: the current roadmap remains the committed `1.12.x` source of truth unless a later roadmap explicitly supersedes it
