# Code Of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and technical without being hostile.
- Assume good faith while reviewing ideas and changes.
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person.
- Share evidence when disagreeing about behavior, risk, or correctness.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- doxxing or disclosure of private information
- deliberate disruption of technical collaboration
- knowingly posting secrets or sensitive exploit details in public

## Maintainer Responsibility

Maintainers may moderate discussions, close abusive threads, and remove contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Use the repo's security or maintainer contact path when conduct issues involve sensitive details.
For normal discussion problems, open a maintainer issue with links and context.
